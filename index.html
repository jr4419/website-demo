What We Treat

Conditions We Treat

Varicose & Spider Veins

Cosmetic and symptomatic vein treatment, including sclerotherapy and laser ablation.

Chronic Venous Insufficiency

Diagnosis and long-term management of leg swelling, heaviness, and skin changes.

Peripheral Artery Disease

Evaluation and treatment of narrowed arteries affecting circulation to the legs.

Deep Vein Thrombosis

Prompt assessment and treatment to reduce clot-related risks and complications.

Dialysis Access

Surgical creation and maintenance of vascular access for dialysis patients.

Carotid Artery Disease

Screening and surgical care to reduce stroke risk from carotid narrowing.

Our Approach

Care that follows the patient, not the protocol.

  • Thorough diagnosis. In-office vascular ultrasound and imaging to find the root cause.
  • Minimally invasive first. We favor outpatient and minimally invasive options when appropriate.
  • Clear communication. You'll understand your diagnosis and every option before deciding.
