Bio

Dr. Thomas Qin

Dr. Qin is a vascular surgeon with 41 years of experience treating conditions affecting the blood vessels throughout the body. He focuses on the surgical and medical management of vascular disease, helping patients with problems related to arteries, veins, and circulation. His extensive experience allows him to provide comprehensive care for complex vascular conditions that require specialized surgical expertise.

Dr. Qin completed his medical education at Shanghai Medical University and his residency training at the University of South Alabama. He practices at multiple locations throughout New York, including affiliations with Beth Israel Medical Center, Lenox Hill Hospital, and Saint John's Episcopal Hospital. His practice locations span from Manhattan to Far Rockaway, providing accessible vascular care across the region.

He speaks Chinese and Spanish in addition to English, enabling him to communicate effectively with diverse patient populations.
